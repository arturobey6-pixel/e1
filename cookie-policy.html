What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners about how visitors use their site. Cookies set by the website owner are called "first-party cookies," while cookies set by other parties (such as analytics providers) are called "third-party cookies."

How We Use Cookies

CleanRide Garage uses cookies and similar technologies on cleanridegarage.com for the following purposes:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function properly, such as remembering your cookie consent choice and enabling core site features. They cannot be switched off.
  • Analytics & Performance Cookies (Google Analytics): We use Google Analytics (measurement ID G-0LY0HY7L01) to collect anonymized information about how visitors use our website — such as which pages are visited most often and how visitors move through the site. This helps us improve our website. These cookies do not directly identify you.
  • Advertising Cookies: If we run online advertising campaigns (for example through Google Ads), third-party vendors including Google may use cookies to measure ad performance and, where you have consented, to show you relevant ads based on your prior visits to this website.

Cookies We Use

The main cookies you may encounter on our website include:

  • _ga, _ga_* — Google Analytics cookies used to distinguish visitors and sessions. Duration: up to 2 years.
  • _gid — Google Analytics cookie used to distinguish visitors. Duration: 24 hours.
  • Consent preference cookies — remember whether you have accepted or declined non-essential cookies. Duration: up to 12 months.

Your Choices & Managing Cookies

You are in control of cookies. Most web browsers allow you to view, manage, delete, and block cookies through your browser settings. You can also opt out of Google Analytics across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, and manage personalized advertising preferences at Google Ads Settings. Please note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience on our website.

If you are visiting from a region that requires consent for non-essential cookies (such as the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom), non-essential cookies will only be used with your consent, and you may withdraw your consent at any time.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies.
